## PRICING INFORMATION AND POLICIES
### Self-Pay Discount
Patients paying without insurance receive an automatic 30% discount from listed prices.
### Payment Plans
Interest-free payment plans available for procedures over $500. Contact Patient Financial Services: (555) 1
### Insurance
Most major insurance plans accepted. Actual out-of-pocket costs vary based on:
- Deductible amount
- Co-insurance percentage
- Co-payment requirements
- In-network vs. out-of-network status
### Estimate Requests
For accurate cost estimates based on your insurance:
- Call: (555) 123-4570
- Email: billing@hospital.org
- Allow 3-5 business days for estimate
### Pre-Authorization
Some tests require insurance pre-authorization. Our staff will assist with this process.
### Results Turnaround Time
**Laboratory Tests:**
- Routine: 24-48 hours
- Specialized: 3-7 days
- Genetic testing: 2-4 weeks
**Imaging:**
- X-Ray: Same day or next day
- CT/MRI: 24-48 hours
- Special studies: 2-3 days
### Access to Results
- Patient Portal: Available 24-48 hours after completion
- By phone: Call (555) 123-4580
- In-person: Medical Records Department
