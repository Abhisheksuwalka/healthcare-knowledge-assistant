HOSPITAL VISITING HOURS AND VISITOR POLICY
Effective Date: November 2024
1. GENERAL VISITING HOURS
1.1 Medical-Surgical Units
- Visiting Hours: 8:00 AM - 8:00 PM daily
- Maximum 2 visitors at bedside at one time
- Visitors must be 12 years or older
1.2 Intensive Care Unit (ICU)
- Visiting Hours: 10:00 AM - 12:00 PM and 4:00 PM - 6:00 PM
- Only 1 visitor at a time
- Visitors must be 18 years or older
- 15-minute visits recommended
- Call ICU nursing station before visiting: (555) 123-4580
1.3 Emergency Department
- Visiting Hours: Limited to 1 family member
- Subject to staff discretion based on patient condition
- May be restricted during critical procedures
1.4 Maternity Ward
- Partners: 24-hour access for birthing partners
- Other Visitors: 2:00 PM - 8:00 PM
- Siblings under 12: 3:00 PM - 6:00 PM only
1.5 Pediatrics Ward
- Parents/Guardians: 24-hour access
- Other Visitors: 10:00 AM - 8:00 PM
- Maximum 2 visitors at bedside
- Siblings must be healthy (no cold/flu symptoms)
2. VISITOR REGISTRATION
2.1 Check-In Process
- All visitors must check in at the Information Desk
- Visitor pass will be issued
- Photo ID required for ICU and Pediatrics
- Visitor log maintained for security
2.2 Visitor Passes
- Must be worn and visible at all times
- Returned upon leaving hospital
- Different colors for different units
3. VISITOR RESTRICTIONS
3.1 Who Cannot Visit
- Children under 12 (except Pediatrics and Maternity siblings)
- Individuals with symptoms of contagious illness
- Persons under influence of alcohol or drugs
- Anyone behaving disruptively
3.2 Restricted Situations
- During medical procedures
- Code situations or emergencies
- Patient isolation (infection control)
- At patient's request
4. VISITOR GUIDELINES
4.1 General Guidelines
- Wash hands before and after visiting
- Keep noise level low
- Turn cell phones to silent/vibrate
- Do not sit on patient beds
- Respect other patients' privacy
- No photography/video without permission
4.2 Food and Beverages
- Outside food allowed for patients (unless restricted)
- No food in ICU waiting areas
- Cafeteria available: Ground Floor, 6:00 AM - 8:00 PM
- Vending machines on each floor
4.3 Smoking Policy
- Hospital is 100% smoke-free
- No smoking anywhere on campus
- Smoking cessation resources available
5. SPECIAL CIRCUMSTANCES
5.1 End-of-Life Care
- Flexible visiting for family members
- Private family rooms available
- Spiritual care services available 24/7
- Support services for families
5.2 Quarantine/Isolation
- Strict visitor limitations
- PPE required (provided by hospital)
- Instructions given by nursing staff
- May be limited to 1 designated visitor
5.3 VIP/Private Rooms
- Standard visiting policies apply
- May request visitor restrictions
- Enhanced privacy available
6. OVERNIGHT STAYS
6.1 Eligible for Overnight Stay
- Parents of pediatric patients
- Birthing partners in Maternity
- Family of ICU patients (case-by-case basis)
6.2 Accommodation
- Recliner chairs available
- Blankets and pillows provided
- Must follow quiet hours: 10:00 PM - 7:00 AM
7. WAITING AREAS
7.1 Facilities
- Comfortable seating
- WiFi access available
- Televisions
- Bathrooms
- Vending machines
7.2 Locations
- Main Lobby Waiting Area: 24/7
- ICU Family Lounge: 3rd Floor
- Surgical Waiting Room: 2nd Floor
- Emergency Department Waiting: Ground Floor
8. COMMUNICATION WITH MEDICAL STAFF
8.1 Updates
- Physicians will update family at designated times
- Nurses available for questions during visiting hours
- Discharge planning involves family
8.2 Designated Contact Person
- Patient may designate one contact for medical updates
- HIPAA-compliant information sharing
- Contact person information updated daily
9. SAFETY AND SECURITY
9.1 Security Measures
- Security personnel on duty 24/7
- Surveillance cameras in public areas
- Panic buttons in patient rooms
- Visitor log maintained
9.2 Reporting Concerns
- Report suspicious activity to Security: (555) 123-4500
- Patient Safety Hotline: (555) 123-4599
10. SPECIAL ACCOMMODATIONS
10.1 Accessibility
- Wheelchair accessible throughout
- Sign language interpreters available
- Assistive listening devices available
10.2 Service Animals
- Service animals permitted
- Must have proper identification
- Emotional support animals: case-by-case basis
